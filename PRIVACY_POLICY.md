# Privacy Policy

_Last Updated: May 2026_

---

## Overview

Pixel Bug Beta runs locally on your device.

The app does not include:
- analytics
- telemetry
- advertisements
- accounts
- cloud sync
- background uploads
- remote tracking

Pixel Bug Beta does not automatically send your artwork, projects, imports, or exports anywhere.

---

## Local Storage

Pixel Bug Beta uses local storage on your device for features such as:

- autosave recovery
- palette presets
- layout arrangements
- tool preferences
- recent session state

This information stays on your machine.

---

## Saving Projects

Autosave is intended for temporary recovery and convenience.

To permanently keep artwork or animation projects, use:

- **File → Save**
- or the keyboard shortcut:
  - `Ctrl/Cmd + S`

Saved project files use the `.pxbuild` format and can be reopened later using:

- **File → Open**
- or:
  - `Ctrl/Cmd + O`

If the application is uninstalled or local app storage is cleared, autosave data may be removed.

Separate saved `.pxbuild` project files are not affected unless you delete them yourself.

---

## Imported Images

Images imported into the pixelizer are processed locally inside the app.

Imported images are not uploaded by Pixel Bug Beta.

---

## Exports

PNG files, GIFs, spritesheets, and exported project files are only created when you manually choose to save or export them.

Exported files are written only to locations you select.

---

## Clearing Data

Uninstalling the application or clearing app storage may remove:
- autosave data
- layout settings
- palette presets
- stored preferences

Separate exported files and manually saved `.pxbuild` projects will remain unless manually deleted.
